Welcome!

Hi, Welcome to my website!
Here I will tell you a little bit about myself and my experience in the world of instructional development. My goal is to inspire you to know that you can pursue your goals, no matter what age or other responsibilities you have in your life. It is never too late to discover your passion and pursue it. I worked in a job for almost two decades that was not fulfilling in the least but it provided for my family and offered stability. Without a college degree, I felt stuck. That is, until I took on a part time job helping out a friend working in online learning systems. I worked that job part time for 8 years before I took the leap and followed my dreams and made it a full time reality. It has been 3 years since then and I have not had one single regret.

"I did what I knew how to do and when I knew better, I did better." ~Maya Angelou
